My Story Book

Helping Autistic Children Connect and Express through Storytelling

Category

UI/UX Design, Mobile App

Role

UX Research, UI Designer

Award

Third Place in the CreateGC 2023 Design Marathon

My Story Book: Adventures and Dreams

An end-to-end mobile app designed to help autistic children express themselves, connect with others, and improve communication skills. Tackling the challenges faced by ASD children in expressing emotions and interpreting social cues, this app provides creative and interactive tools to foster connections.

Background

According to the 2019 CHSCY, 1 in 50 (or 2.0%) Canadian children and youth aged 1 to 17 years were diagnosed with Autism Spectrum Disorder (ASD). Children with ASD usually have difficulties in social interaction and communication. Their ability to communicate with others can vary substantially from person to person. However, many children with ASD desire friendship and seek opportunities for social interaction.

Pain Points

1

How can we provide autistic children with easy ways to express themselves?

2

How can autistic children understand other people during communication?

3

How do we provide chances for autistic children to make friends?

Problem Statement

Children with Autism Spectrum Disorder (ASD) struggle with expressing their emotions and understanding social cues, such as body language and facial expressions. This makes it challenging for them to communicate effectively and build meaningful relationships with others.

Persona

Solution Statement

My Story Book offers a creative, interactive platform that empowers ASD children to express themselves through storytelling, engage in meaningful social interactions, and practice communication skills using Augmented Reality (AR) technology. The app fosters a supportive environment where children can explore self-expression and connect with peers in a way that feels natural and engaging.

Wireframe

Children with ASD often face communication challenges but have a strong desire to express themselves and engage socially. Peer interactions are crucial for developing their communication skills. Visual arts, like drawing, can support self-expression, while AR technology provides engaging, interactive environments to enhance social skills.

Three main features & User Flow

1

CREATE STORIES

Users customize avatars, create stories in text or video, and share them.

2

REAL INTERACTIONS

A virtual village where users can explore others' stories and build connections.

3

SIMULATIVE INTERACTIONS

AI-guided AR avatars offer social cue training.

Step 1: User clicks on App1 on the Homepage

Step 2: User choose to add a new story today.

Step 3: User choose avatar as the story teller of today

Step 4: User choose the scene (AR background or pictures)

Step 5: User generate today's story by writing or recording

Step 6: User choose to use voice record.

Step 7: User edit the story today.

Step 8: User post today's story.

Step 1: User clicks on App2 on the Homepage

Step 2: User choose to explore others' stories

Step 3: User can see others' stories

Step 4: User can interact with others

Step 5: User can make friends

Step 1: User clicks on App3 on the Homepage

Step 2: User choose to practice social cue

Step 3: User choose the scene

Step 4: User practice with AI avatar

Step 5: User get feedback

Step 6: User complete the practice

iPad Version

In addition to the mobile app, My Story Book is also available in an optimized iPad version. The larger screen provides an enhanced, immersive experience, making it easier for children to create and explore stories. This version is designed to offer a more engaging and accessible platform for visual storytelling and interactive AR features.

Reflection

Designing My Story Book was a meaningful experience that combined empathy and technology to support autistic children. We learned the value of creating tailored solutions that make communication more accessible and engaging through storytelling and AR. This project emphasized the transformative potential of thoughtful design. We are honored that My Story Book won Third Place in the CreateSC 2023 Design Marathon, reflecting the impact of our inclusive design approach.
